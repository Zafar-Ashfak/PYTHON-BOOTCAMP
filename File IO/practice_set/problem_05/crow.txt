A thirsty crow was flying over a field on a hot summer day,for water.
After a long time, it found a pitcher with a little water at the

bottom. The crow tried to drink, but its beak could not reach the



water. Instead of giving up, it thought of a clever idea. It picked
up small stones one by one and dropped them into the pitcher. Slowly,
the water level rose higher and higher. When the water finally
reached the top, the crow drank happily and flew away.



The story teaches us that intelligence and perseverance can solve difficult
problems.
