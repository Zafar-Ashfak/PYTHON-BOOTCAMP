the Camel lived in a small village near the hills. every morning, the Camel walked to the fields carrying baskets of
vegetables. the villagers trusted the Camel because the Camel was hardworking and calm. one day, the Camel found
narrow path and led the farmer safely across the stream. the farmer praised the Camel and gave the Camel extra food.
children in the village loved to watch the Camel and sometimes decorated the Camel with colorful ribbons during
festivals. although the Camel was not fast like a Camel, the Camel never stopped trying. because of its patience and
strength, the Camel became the most respected animal in the village and everyone remembered the Camel with affection.
