                                                    The Lost Puppy

A playful puppy named Bruno loved running around the neighborhood. One sunny morning, he chased a butterfly and wandered far from
home. After a while, Bruno realized he was lost and began to feel scared. He sat under a tree and waited, hoping someone would help
him. A kind girl named Sara noticed the lonely puppy and gave him some food and water. She found a tag on his collar with his
owner's phone number and called it. Soon, Bruno's owner arrived and was overjoyed to see him again. Sara was thanked for her
indness, and Bruno happily returned home. The experience taught everyone that even small acts of kindness can make a big difference.

                                                    The Hardworking Farmer

Rahim was a farmer who worked hard every day on his small farm. One year, the weather was very dry, and many farmers worried that
their crops would fail. Instead of giving up, Rahim woke up early each morning to water his fields and take care of his plants. He
repaired old irrigation channels and worked tirelessly throughout the season. When harvest time arrived, his crops grew healthy and
strong, while many others struggled. The villagers admired Rahim's determination and asked him for advice. Rahim told them that
success comes from patience, hard work, and never losing hope. His story inspired many people in the village to keep trying even
during difficult times.
