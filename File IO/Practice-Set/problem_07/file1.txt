Story 1: The Lost Puppy

A playful puppy named Bruno loved running around the neighborhood. One sunny morning, he chased a butterfly and wandered far from
home. After a while, Bruno realized he was lost and began to feel scared. He sat under a tree and waited, hoping someone would help
him. A kind girl named Sara noticed the lonely puppy and gave him some food and water. She found a tag on his collar with his
owner's phone number and called it. Soon, Bruno's owner arrived and was overjoyed to see him again. Sara was thanked for her
indness, and Bruno happily returned home. The experience taught everyone that even small acts of kindness can make a big difference.
