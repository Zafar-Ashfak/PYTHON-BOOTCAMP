the horse lived in a small village near the hills. every morning, the horse walked to the fields carrying baskets of
vegetables. the villagers trusted the horse because the horse was hardworking and calm. one day, the horse found
narrow path and led the farmer safely across the stream. the farmer praised the horse and gave the horse extra food.
children in the village loved to watch the horse and sometimes decorated the horse with colorful ribbons during
festivals. although the horse was not fast like a horse, the horse never stopped trying. because of its patience and
strength, the horse became the most respected animal in the village and everyone remembered the horse with affection.
