A poor woodcutter lived near a forest and earned his living by cutting trees. One day, while working near a river, his axe slipped from his hands and fell into the water. The woodcutter became very sad because he could not afford a new axe. Suddenly, a fairy appeared and offered to help. She brought out a golden axe and asked if it was his. The woodcutter honestly said no. Then she showed him a silver axe, but again he said it was not his. Finally, she brought out his old iron axe, and he happily claimed it. Impressed by his honesty, the fairy rewarded him with all three axes. The story teaches us that honesty is always rewarded.
